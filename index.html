Welcome to Camp ReJoyce!


Our Mission Statement

The purpose of this camp is to learn God's Word, to worship in the Spirit, and to draw us closer to God and closer to one another. We believe the unsaved will be touched and moved to accept Jesus into their hearts. God will touch all participants and fill them with the Spirit of God.


About Camp ReJoyce


What is Camp ReJoyce?

Camp ReJoyce is not your average church camp. The focal point of our camp is Christ. We focus on learning the Word of God, and diving deeper in your relationship with Christ. Our desire for all campers if for them to know the love of Christ for them and His desire to lead their lives.


Who is Camp ReJoyce for?

Everyone! Well, almost everyone. Kids ages 9-18 are welcome to Camp ReJoyce! Don't worry, you don't have to be a Christian to attend camp! All we ask is campers come with an open heart and mind, and a respectful attitude to everyone there.

Got a child under 9? There are some exceptions! Please reach out to see if we can make an exception for your child.

Older than 18? Camp ReJoyce is always looking for volunteers. Contact us for more information on how you can be involved!
