Camp ReJoyce!

Mark your calendars! Camp ReJoyce will be held from July 8th-11th at the Gilmer County Recreation Center in Glenville, WV (check out our location page for more details about the camp grounds). Camp ReJoyce is an overnight camp, so all campers will need to bring clothes and sleeping gear for the week (more details on what to and not to bring on the schedule page). Check-in and Registration are on Tuesday July 8th from 10:00-11:00 at Camp, and the Closing Ceremony is Friday July 11th at 10:00. Parents are welcome to come to the Closing Ceremony on Friday. All parents must be at camp to pick up their camper at 11:00. Contact us for information on rides to and from camp.

Camp ReJoyce Church Takeover!

8095 Hazlewood Ave, Stonewood, WV 26301
