    General Camp Rules

  1. Keep the grounds clean
  2. Respect all camp properties
  3. Be a good neighbor
  4. All meals must be attended unless excused by a counselor
  5. Istructions given by Counselors or Leaders must be respected by all campers
  6. Campers will be permitted to call home only in case of an emergency. Cell phones, if brought, must be turned in to the director upon registration. Visible cell phones will be confiscated
  7. Any camper showing an unchristian attitude or committing an infraction of the rules will be referred to the Discipline Committee to determine if the camper should continue or be dismissed from the camp
  8. Smoking or the use of any other form of vapes, tobacco, drugs, alcoholic beverages, or the use of foul language will not be tolerated on the campgrounds
  9. Name-calling, teasing, and bullying will not be tolerated

    10. Each camper should maintain a good disposition and willingness to participate

Dress Code

  • Modest, decent, practical, and comfortable clothing
  • No short shorts, crop tops, spaghetti strap tank tops, tight fitting or revealing clothing
  • Clothing with rips or holes in revealing places is not permitted
  • No midriff-revealing tops allowed
  • Sturdy shoes must me worn during sports to prevent injury
  • Shorts, skirts, and dresses must be fingertip length or longer
  • Dress in a neat, clean, and decent manner
  • Tank top straps must be 3 finger wide
  • No clothes with inappropriate messages or profanity including: pictures, slogans, or anything with alcohol, drugs, or sexual innuendos depicted or referenced is strictly prohibited
